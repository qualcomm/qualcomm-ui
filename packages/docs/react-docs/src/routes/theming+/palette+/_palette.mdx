There's a science to color. It's part visual chemistry and part psychology. Balancing both makes for a palette that's a powerful tool for establishing uniqueness.

### Brand

The brand color is most frequently used across your UI, and imparts a distinct brand identity to the product. 500 is the base color for all colors, `brand-500` represents the primary, `brand-300` the secondary and `brand-100` the tertiary color. Other brand shades should be used sparingly.
